Lecture Room

There are many lecture rooms within the John Dalton Building. These rooms provide the optimal learning experience for students as each room is fitted with large board projectors, small paper projectors, white boards, comfortable seating and desks for papers, books and laptops. These rooms can be found on all lower and upper floors in the East side of the building.
