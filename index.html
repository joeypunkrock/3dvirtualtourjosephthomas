The John Dalton Bulding

Manchester Metropoliton University's John Dalton building is home to the School of Computing, Mathematics and Digital Technology.

It is extremely well located, on Chester Street and Oxford Road, as well as being a 5 minute walk away from the Oxford Road Train Station.

The John Dalton building is a large modern building and is famous for "The Street", a large, ground floor, contemporary walkway which links the two parts of the building.
